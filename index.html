The method

Recovery, treated like a system you can study

Three mechanisms work together: honest daily logging, pattern recognition across your own data, and structured feedback that targets causes — not willpower alone.

Log without friction

Two buttons, one decision a day. Removing barriers to honest tracking is itself a behavioral intervention — the easier the log, the more truthful it stays.

Find the pattern

After a few entries, the system looks for recurring triggers, risky hours, and emotional states — turning scattered moments into a legible map of your own behavior.

Act on the cause

Insight only matters if it changes the next decision. You get specific, situational suggestions — not generic willpower slogans — for the conditions you actually face.

    Privacy

    MAAR NO FAP was built on one rule: this is your data, not ours.

    • No accounts, sign-ups, or logins required, ever.
    • No advertisements and no tracking of any kind.
    • No servers — your recovery data is never uploaded anywhere.
    • Everything is stored directly on your own device, in a folder you choose and control.
    • You can export, back up, or delete your data at any time — no one else has a copy.
